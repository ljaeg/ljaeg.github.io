My Work

HRL Laboratories, 11/2021-02/2026

I worked on the quantum program at HRL Laboratories in Malibu, California. HRL's quantum program is at the forefront of quantum science and engineering. I was primarily focused on boosting the yield of HRL's semiconductor quantum dot devices by finding improvements in the design and fabrication processes, leading in-depth studies of device electrical properties, and applying physics-based mathematical models to better understand device failure mechanisms. You can read more about HRL's quantum program here. A paper on which I am a co-author is forthcoming.

Space Sciences Lab, 05/2019-07/2021

As an undergraduate, I worked at the Space Sciences Lab in Berkeley, California, under Dr. Andrew Westphal. Our group was focused on learning about the conditions of the early solar system. I led a project to automate data collection from the NASA Stardust mission with an algorithm based on two-dimensional convolutional neural networks. I detailed these efforts in a first author paper published in the journal Meteoritics and Planetary Science.
