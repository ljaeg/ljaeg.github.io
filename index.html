My Work

As an undergraduate, I worked at the Space Sciences Lab in Berkeley, California, under Dr. Andrew Westphal. Our group was focused on learning about the conditions of the early solar system. I led a project to create an automate and facilitate data collection from the NASA Stardust mission with an algorithm based on a two-dimensional convolutional neural network. I detailed these efforts in a first author paper published in the journal Meteoritics and Planetary Science.

I worked on the quantum program at HRL Laboratories in Malibu, California. HRL's quantum program is at the forefront of quantum science and engineering.
